WHO WE ARE

We are a medium sized lath and plastering contractor located in San Diego, California. We serve the stucco markets of San Diego County, Orange County, and parts of Greater Los Angeles. We strive for customer satisfaction every day in everything we do. We believe that if you take care of your clients, the bottom line takes care of itself. At JP Lath and Plastering, our motto is always “Our Clients Are Our Most Valuable Asset.”
